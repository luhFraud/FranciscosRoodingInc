Re-Roofing

Re-roofing is an alternative to completely removing and replacing a roof. During re-roofing, a new shingle overlay is placed over your worn shingles. Francisco's Roofing Inc makes re-roofing cost-effective by helping the client save on material and labor cost. With Francisco's Roofing Inc we can restore your roof's appearance and provide a new protective layer to help prevent leaks from getting inside your home.

Roof coating

Roof coatings add more benefits to your roof. A roof with a white or reflective coating will help keep the building cooler by reducing temperatures within the building and reducing energy consumptions from air conditioning. Adding a roof coating will also give your roof an extra layer of protection from leaks. With the expertise of Francisco's Roofing Inc we can help you choose a coating and help extend the life of your roof.

different types of roof

Composition shingles

Composition shingles are made up of materials like asphalt, slate, laminate, tar paper, wood and fiberglass. They come in a variety of colors and are a cost-effective long-term option. These shingles can last around 30 to 50 years and require low maintenance.

slate shingles

Slate roof shingles are a natural roofing product made of hard rock known as metamorphic rock which are derived from sedimentary rock of volcanic ash and clay. Slate roof shingles are highly durable and aesthetically pleasing. The durability of a slate roof shingle can last for 50 to 100+ years.

tile

A tile roof is a roof made from clay, ceramic, or concrete tiles. These tiles are very durable and can last for 50 to 100+ years. Tile roof is a very popular style of roof here in Los Angeles area for its beautiful look and aesthetic style.

flat

Here at Francisco's Roofing Inc we use a self-adhering roofing system for our flat or low-slope roof clients. These self-adhering systems are applied without torches, open flames, or hot asphalt. This is a two layered system where we first lay a self-adhering base/ply sheet and lay a layer of self-adhering cap sheet above. This system helps provide durable protection on low-slope area of your home, including porches, garages, carports, and sheds.
